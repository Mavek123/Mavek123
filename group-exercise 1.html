Group Exercise Classes

The Group Exercise section offers a variety of fun and engaging fitness classes designed for individuals looking to stay active while socializing and having fun.

Yoga

Relax, stretch, and improve your flexibility with our calming yoga sessions, suitable for all levels.

Zumba

Get your heart pumping with high-energy Zumba classes. This dance-inspired workout is a fun way to burn calories.

Pilates

Focus on strengthening your core and improving your posture with Pilates.

Cycling

Join our indoor cycling classes for an intense cardiovascular workout.

HIIT

High-Intensity Interval Training offers short bursts of intense exercise followed by brief rest periods.

Circuit Training

This class combines strength and cardio exercises, moving through a series of stations.
